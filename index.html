Event Schedule

With a focus on leadership, collaboration, innovation, and partnership, this year's event will explore on four impactful topics:  

Plenary Session 1: Empowering Filipino Canadians: Leading with Vision, Building with Purpose

Emphasizes visionary leadership and purposeful action to build a brighter future for Filipino-Canadians, fostering community growth and development

Takeaways:

  • Vision-Driven Leadership: Effective leaders have a clear and compelling vision that inspires community engagement and drives collective progress.
  • Purposeful Action: Success is achieved through intentional strategies that address the specific needs of Filipino Canadians.
  • Mentorship Matters: Building a culture of mentorship is essential in nurturing emerging leaders and creating a sustainable leadership pipeline.

Plenary Session 2: Together We Thrive: Championing Filipino Leadership in Canada

Highlights the power of unity, collaboration, and collective achievements in building thriving communities across Canada.

Takeaways:

  • Power of Collaboration: Inclusive and collective leadership fosters stronger, more resilient communities.
  • Networking for Growth: Establishing connections among leaders from various regions amplifies opportunities for knowledge sharing and resource pooling.
  • Community Engagement: Filipino-Canadian leaders thrive when they actively engage and empower their communities to co-create solutions.

Breakout Session 1:

Objective: To explore how Filipino leaders can adopt a visionary approach while taking purposeful actions to drive community development and personal leadership growth.

Discussion Points:

  • Defining visionary leadership in the Filipino-Canadian context
  • Strategies for setting purposeful goals aligned with community needs
  • Building mentorship programs to inspire emerging leaders
  • Sharing success stories of Filipino Canadians leading with impact

Vision Mapping:

Participants will create a strategic vision board for leadership initiatives in their communities.

Plenary Session 3: Trailblazing the Future: Empowering Communities, Driving Change

Objective: Focuses on innovation, progress, and transformative leadership as Filipino Canadians drive positive change and forge new pathways for future generations.

Takeaways:

  • Innovation as a Catalyst: Embracing emerging trends and technological advancements fosters transformative leadership and community solutions.
  • Youth Empowerment: The next generation plays a critical role in shaping the future of Filipino leadership in Canada.
  • Resilience Amid Challenges: Leaders must adapt and remain innovative to navigate complex community and societal challenges.

Plenary Session 4: Catalysts for Change: Building Leadership Capacities and Strategic Alliances

Objective: Centers on equipping Filipino leaders with tools to drive impactful change while fostering partnerships and forming alliances with like-minded organizations.

Takeaways:

  • Strategic Partnerships: Collaborations with non-profits and advocacy groups enhance leadership impact and community reach.
  • Capacity Building: Continuous learning and development strengthen leadership capabilities and foster adaptive leaders.
  • Shared Resources and Goals: Working together with aligned objectives maximizes community benefits and organizational success

Each topic reflects our ongoing commitment to advocating for the Filipino diaspora across Canada while fostering leadership capacities among Filipino Canadians, promoting collaboration and meaningful contributions to society.

Breakout Session 2

Objective: To highlight the importance of collaboration and community-driven initiatives in fostering leadership and unity among Filipino Canadians.

Discussion Points:

  • Identifying collaborative leadership models that work within diverse communities
  • Creating inclusive spaces for collective decision-making
  • Networking strategies to connect leaders across regions
  • Showcasing successful community projects born out of collaboration

Activity:

Collaborative Roundtable: Teams brainstorm and pitch actionable community projects.
